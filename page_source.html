Announcements

New:  The Transparency Website has a new search feature that allows users to view the types of care services provided by each licensee across the following CCLD programs: Adult & Senior Care, Child Care, Children’s Residential, and Home Care Services. This feature is available on the Transparency Website home page under ‘Search by License Name’ field. Users must enter the Exact Licensee Name in the Facility Search by Licensee Name box. The exact names can be located by using the ‘Download Data’ feature located at the top of the page.
New:  The Download Data feature is now available on the Transparency site.
New:  The Transparency Website has a new feature that allows users to view facility license comments called Facility Comment, which is located on the Facility Details page. The Facility Comment feature will be available for Child Care – Infant Center, School Age Child Care Center, Child Care Center, Child Care Center Preschool, Single Licensed Child Center, and Home Care Organization.
New:  If you are searching for care in the impacted area due to the Los Angeles and/or Ventura County Fires, please also reach out to your local Resource and Referral Agency for child care or alternative placement agencies for residential care.
New:  We are working to reflect (860) Single Licensed Childcare Centers, if there is a need to conduct a file review, please contact the local regional office or the Centralized Complaint and Information Bureau @ letusno@dss.ca.gov.
New:  The Transparency Website has a new feature that allows users to search for closed facilities. To search for closed facilities the user will need to enter a 9-digit facility number and click on the search button located below. Otherwise the information can be found in the Download Data feature.
New:  California law has changed. The term “unsubstantiated” will be used for allegations that don’t have enough evidence to prove that they happened. In the past, these were called “inconclusive.” See Provider Information Notice (PIN) 17-02 available under the CCLD section of the website for more information.

Facility Group FAQ
  • Where can I find the facility definitions?
    • See the Glossary link at the top.
  • Where can I find out more about how facilities are regulated in California?
    • Please visit the Community Care Licensing website.
  • How do I file a complaint against a licensee/facility?
    • To file a complaint against a facility licensed by the California Department of Social Services, you can contact our Centralized Complaint and Information Bureau at 1-844-538-8766, or by email at letusno@dss.ca.gov. You can remain anonymous if you wish.
      For additional information regarding filing a complaint, please visit the following webpage: http://www.cdss.ca.gov/inforesources/CCLD-Complaint-Hotline/.
